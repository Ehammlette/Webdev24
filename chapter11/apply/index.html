How to Prepare for an Interview

Do research on your potential employer and prepare answers for the interview.

Always follow up after the interview.

  • Be on time to the interview.
  • Greet the interviewer with a handshake and a smile.
  • Have a reliable pen and a small note pad with you.
  • Don’t expect a job offer at the first interview.
  • No interview is complete until you follow up with a thank-you note.
